I started making this thinking it would take an hour. One hour. That's it.

Then I changed the colours. Then the notes. Then rewrote this letter twice. Then stared at the screen wondering if any of it was actually good enough for you.

So if this looks a little overdone — it's because I genuinely couldn't stop until it felt right.

It's kind of wild how someone who entered my life so randomly on 5th May 2026 ended up becoming such a big part of it.

Thank you. For every story. Every laugh. Every random conversation at weird hours. Every "Aur bata…" — and even every "Yaar bas ye mat puch."

You already know what I feel about you. But what you probably don't know is how nervous I was on 21st June. I genuinely went completely blank. I had so much to say and I couldn't say any of it.
